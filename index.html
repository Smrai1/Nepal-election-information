How to Vote in Nepal

1

Check Registration

Verify your name in voter list:

  • Age 18+ years
  • Nepali citizenship certificate
2

Go to Polling Station

Visit your polling station:

  • Time: 7 AM to 5 PM
  • Bring citizenship certificate
3

Cast Your Vote

Inside voting booth:

  • Receive ballot paper
  • Mark your choice secretly
  • Fold and drop in ballot box
